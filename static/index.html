Product

Choose a currency to continue. Customers and subscriptions are created on the master account. The selected currency determines the processing account used for orchestration and (optionally) payment.

What happens next?

You'll be taken to the customer form. The address is collected using Stripe Address Element, then the backend creates a Stripe Customer on the correct account and sets metadata Processing_Account.
